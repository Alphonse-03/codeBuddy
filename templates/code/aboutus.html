Our Aim:

Codebuddy.com is an initiative to help people come from different backgrounds and skillsets to get social and code in a group and grow not as an individual but as a team. This initiative will inculcate the spirit of group work and will promote self-taught developers to get a peer support for free. We have lot of Startups hiring for young talent where you can just apply with a single tap so it's no hassle to go through the tedious task of applying.
